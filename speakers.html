Meet the speakers
Get to know more about the speakers, their work and process, and ask questions!
Casey Reas
A computational artist and co-developer of Processing, a programming language geared towards the visual arts. His conceptual, procedural and minimal artworks explore ideas through the contemporary lens of software. He has featured work in numerous museums and galleries in the United States, Europe and Asia.
Jay Wong
A user experience designer with a passion for creating beautiful experiences that connect humans with technology, and teaching others to love design as much she does. She has worked as the Design Lead on Project Fi, Google’s wireless carrier service, and started Omnomnom Studios, a creative collective that builds apps for large companies.
Noah Levin
A Senior Interaction Designer at Google who led the design of the iOS Google Search app with voice search and Google Now. He also lead prototyping workshops regularly for start ups, agencies, and large companies. He received both his undergraduate and master’s degrees at Carnegie Mellon in Human-Computer Interaction.
